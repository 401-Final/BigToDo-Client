Welcome to the BigToDo App!

BigToDo is more than a to-do list! Some of the reasons to-do lists cause stress are:

  • They don't contain enough (if you only write down the most important things)
  • They display too much (you should capture everything, but that doesn't mean you should see it all the time)
  • They don't filter to-dos by where you are and what you have available to work with
